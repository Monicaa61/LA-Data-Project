Explanation

Our project is about the water usage according to zip codes. The water usage is measured by gallons. This makes it visible to see where the water usage can be reduced. California is known for having droughts and knowing where water is used more can help reduce the water usage. Our project contains the water usage in gallons, the zipcode, and the year. Informing others about water usage could create awareness allowing for people to try to improve their water saving methods.
